Rendering Reality: Research Areas

>> FOCUS: Plasmonic & Heterogeneous Catalysis
Investigating reaction pathways on noble metal surfaces enhanced by localised surface plasmon resonance (LSPR).

>> FOCUS: Novel Architectures
Calculating band structures of novel elemental allotropes using Density Functional Theory (DFT).

Theoretical Framework:
We employ the Kohn-Sham formulation for many-body systems:

$$ \left( -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \nabla^2 + V_{eff}(\mathbf{r}) \right) \psi_i(\mathbf{r}) = \varepsilon_i \psi_i(\mathbf{r}) $$

>> FOCUS: Dynamics of Biomimetic Systems
Using QM/MM approaches to understand the enzymatic activity and proton transfer mechanisms in biological environments.
