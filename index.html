01. ABOUT THE LAB

Welcome to the AND Lab. We operate at the intersection of theoretical physics and computational chemistry. Our work utilizes high-performance computing (HPC) to model Valleytronics, Topological Materials, and Complex Catalytic Systems.

02. RESEARCH AREAS

>> FOCUS: Plasmonic & Heterogeneous Catalysis
Investigating reaction pathways on noble metal surfaces enhanced by localized surface plasmon resonance (LSPR).

>> FOCUS: Boron Architectures
Calculating band structures of novel boron allotropes using Density Functional Theory (DFT).

Theoretical Framework:
We employ the Kohn-Sham formulation for many-body systems:

$$ \left( -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \nabla^2 + V_{eff}(\mathbf{r}) \right) \psi_i(\mathbf{r}) = \varepsilon_i \psi_i(\mathbf{r}) $$
